Wedding Card Design Styles

Explore the different styles of wedding invitations to find what best represents your love story

Traditional Elegance

Classic designs featuring formal script fonts, intricate borders, and luxurious materials like heavyweight cotton paper with foil stamping. Perfect for black-tie weddings and traditional ceremonies.

  • Formal script typography
  • Foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold)
  • Layered paper designs

Contemporary Minimalist

Clean, sophisticated designs with ample white space, geometric elements, and modern typography. Ideal for couples who appreciate simplicity and contemporary aesthetics.

  • Sans-serif fonts and clean lines
  • Subtle color palettes
  • Unique paper cuts and folds

Rustic Charm

Earthy, natural designs featuring kraft paper, twine accents, and floral illustrations. Perfect for barn weddings, outdoor ceremonies, and nature-inspired celebrations.

  • Natural paper textures
  • Hand-drawn botanical elements
  • Burlap or lace accents

Luxury Embellished

Opulent designs featuring premium materials like silk, velvet, or handmade paper with sophisticated embellishments for a truly luxurious presentation.

  • Hand-calligraphed details
  • Crystal or pearl embellishments
  • Custom wax seals

Destination Wedding

Vibrant designs that capture the essence of your wedding location, whether it's a tropical beach, European city, or mountain retreat.

  • Location-specific motifs
  • Vibrant color schemes
  • Travel-themed embellishments

Fully Custom

Completely original designs created from scratch to match your unique vision. We'll work closely with you to bring your dream invitation to life.

  • Personalized illustrations
  • Unique materials and finishes
  • One-of-a-kind design elements

Our Design Process

How we create your perfect wedding invitations, from concept to delivery

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your vision, wedding theme, color palette, and personal style. This can be done via video call, in-person, or through our detailed questionnaire.

  • Discuss your wedding theme and colors
  • Review design preferences and inspiration
  • Determine budget and timeline
2

Concept Development

Our designers create 2-3 unique concepts based on your preferences. You'll receive digital mockups showing different design directions, color options, and typography choices.

  • Receive initial design concepts within 5 business days
  • Option to request physical samples of paper stocks and finishes
  • Unlimited revisions until perfect
3

Refinement

Once you select a concept direction, we refine every detail - from paper weight and texture to printing techniques and special finishes. You'll approve a final proof before production begins.

  • Customize all text and wording
  • Select from premium materials and finishes
  • Finalize all design elements
4

Production & Delivery

Our artisans handcraft your invitations using the finest materials and printing techniques. Each piece is carefully inspected before being packaged and shipped to you or your guests.

  • Standard production takes 10-14 business days
  • Rush services available
  • Worldwide shipping options
